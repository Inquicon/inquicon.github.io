üí° Pro Tips

  • Think Outside the Box: Don't just use text-based challenges. Consider visual patterns, audio cues, character recognition, trope identification, or even mini-games!
  • Test with Friends: Make sure anime fans can actually solve it in 30 seconds. If your otaku friends struggle, simplify it!
  • Anime Everything: From the UI design to the challenge itself, make sure every element screams anime!
  • Explain Well: Your explanation is crucial. Make it clear why your design is clever and effective.
  • Keep it Fun: Remember, this should be enjoyable for users while being secure against bots!
