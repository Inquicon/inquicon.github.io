üìã Submission Requirements

Minimum Panel Count

At least 25 panels OR 1 complete chapter

If your chapter is less than 25 panels, you must submit a complete chapter that tells a cohesive story.

Your submission must include:

  • Original story and concept
  • Clear panel layout and flow
  • Readable text/dialogue (if applicable)
  • A title for your manga
  • Brief synopsis (50-100 words)

üìú Rules & Guidelines

  • Original Work Only: Your manga must be your own original creation. No plagiarism or tracing.
  • Format: Submit in PDF, PNG, or JPG format. Make sure panels are in the correct reading order.
  • Language: Manga can be in any language, but please provide an English translation/summary if not in English.
  • Content: Keep it appropriate! No excessive violence, NSFW content, or hate speech.
  • AI Art Declaration: You must clearly state if AI was used in your submission.
  • One Entry Per Person: You may only submit one manga for the competition.
  • Rights: You retain rights to your work, but Inquicon may showcase winning entries on social media with credit.
