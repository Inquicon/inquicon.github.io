üìú Rules & Guidelines

  • Any Character Welcome: Anime, manga, video games, or any pop culture character!
  • Appropriate Content: Keep it PG-13. No excessively revealing or inappropriate costumes.
  • Safety First: Props must be con-safe (no real weapons, sharp objects, or dangerous materials).
  • Original Work Encouraged: While purchased cosplays are allowed, handmade efforts will be noted!
  • Be Respectful: Respect fellow participants, judges, and audience members.
  • Have Fun: This is about celebrating your passion‚Äîenjoy the experience!
  • Stage Time: Keep performances within reasonable time limits (guidelines will be provided at the event).

üí° Cosplay Tips

  • Practice Your Poses: Know your character's signature poses and expressions!
  • Bring Reference Images: Show judges your reference material to highlight accuracy.
  • Plan Your Performance: Think about how to showcase your cosplay in your stage time.
  • Test Your Costume: Make sure you can move, sit, and perform comfortably in your outfit.
  • Prepare Props Properly: Secure any props so they're easy to handle on stage.
  • Stay In Character: Embody your character from the moment you step on stage!
  • Document Your Work: Take progress photos if you made your cosplay‚Äîjudges love to see the process!
