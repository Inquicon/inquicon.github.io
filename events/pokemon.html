üìä Tournament Format
Round 1: Initial Battles
All N registered participants battle against each other. Winners advance, creating N/2 winners.
Round 2: Second Chance Selection
Random losers from Round 1 are selected and added to the N/2 winners to make the total a power of 2 (e.g., 8, 16, 32, 64 participants).
Bracket Stage: Elimination Rounds
Standard single-elimination bracket continues until we have our Winner and Runner-up.
MVP Selection
The audience votes for the Most Valuable Player (MVP) based on skill, strategy, and performance throughout the tournament!

üéØ How to Prepare

Step 1: Create Your Pokemon Showdown Account

  • Go to play.pokemonshowdown.com
  • Click "Choose Name" and register an account
  • Verify your email if required

Step 2: Build Your Team

  • Click on "Teambuilder" in Pokemon Showdown
  • Select "Gen 9 OU" or appropriate format
  • Build a team of 6 Pokemon following the tournament rules
  • Remember: NO Legendaries, Ultra Beasts, Mega Evolution, Dynamax, or OHKO moves!
  • Test your team in practice battles

Step 3: Practice & Strategize

  • Practice battles on Showdown to refine your strategy
  • Learn type matchups and move interactions
  • Prepare for different team compositions
  • Don't forget - Terastallize is allowed!
