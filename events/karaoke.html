üìú Participation Guidelines

  • No Sign-Up Required: Just show up and let us know you want to perform!
  • Keep It Short: Try to keep performances under 3-4 minutes (one full song)
  • Be Ready: Know your song choice before stepping up
  • Respectful Audience: Cheer for all performers - everyone's here to have fun!
  • Language: Japanese, English, or any language - all welcome!
  • No Judging: This is not a competition - just pure fun!
  • Stage Presence: Dance, pose, or just stand and sing - it's up to you!

üí° Performance Tips

  • Choose a Song You Know: Pick something you're comfortable with
  • Practice the Lyrics: At least know the general flow, even if you mess up some words
  • Have Fun with It: Don't take it too seriously - enjoy yourself!
  • Hydrate: Drink water before performing to keep your voice fresh
  • Project Confidence: Even if you're nervous, fake it till you make it!
  • Engage the Crowd: Make eye contact, smile, and feel the energy!
  • Don't Worry About Mistakes: Everyone makes them - just keep going!
